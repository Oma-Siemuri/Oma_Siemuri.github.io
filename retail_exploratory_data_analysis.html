Project: Sales Data Cleaning and Exploratory Data Analysis¶

Industry Data: Electronics Hardware Retailer Sales¶

Objective:¶

To explore the data for insights and demo how data can help answer questions such as:¶

  • What time should we display advertisement to maximize the likelihood of customers conversion?
  • What Products are most often sold together?
  • Which product sold the most and why?
  • Month of the year with best sales
  • Sales by City Location

Project Plan¶

1. The data is located in 12 separate csv files for each month of sales -> Combine the dataset for ease of handling.¶

2. Perform data cleaning and transformation to facilitate EDA¶

3. Explore the data for insights listed above¶
